You find yourself standing at the bustling docks of Eldoria, a medieval city bathed in the warm glow of the setting sun. The salty sea breeze fills the air as ships bob gently in the harbor. Your mission is clear - to hunt down a thief who stole a precious portrait of the Queen. You start your investigation by strolling along the docks, observing the activity. To your left, fishermen unload their catch, and to your right, merchants haggle over exotic goods. A shady figure catches your eye near the alley. What will you do?
